Our "Origin Story" 🍔

Every legend starts somewhere. Ours started with heartburn.

Fat Henry's was founded by a guy who loved burgers so much he made it a personality trait. While other kids had hobbies, Henry had a grill and a dream. That dream? To build burgers so absurdly stacked that you'd need a game plan just to eat one.

There's no MBA. No business plan. No exit strategy. Just obscene amounts of beef, questionable levels of cheese, and the unshakeable belief that calories don't count if you're having fun.

We don't do "light options." We don't do "sensible portions." We do burgers that make your cardiologist send a strongly-worded letter. And we do them with love. 🍔❤️
